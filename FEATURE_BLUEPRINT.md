# KIDORA Quest v5 Feature Blueprint

## Pedagogical Cycle
Hear → Move → Say → Read → Create → Share

## Main Areas
1. SoundQuest AI: listening, story audio, clue audio, word audio, command audio, stop audio, listening evidence.
2. EchoMirror AI: model sentence, voice recording, playback, gentle feedback, dialogue practice, speaking evidence.
3. GlyphPath AI: phonics, word garden, sentence path, mini story, picture-word matching, reading evidence.
4. StoryForge AI: drawing canvas, tracing guide, sentence train, read sentence, writing evidence, storybook page.
5. Puzzle Bridge: integrated four-skill mission.
6. WordSpark Lab: memory match and fast word play.
7. Living Storybook: saved voice, drawing, reading, writing, and story evidence.
8. Treasure Room: badges, stars, XP, printable passport.

## Built-In Themes
Colours, Animals, Food, Family, Body, Weather, Classroom, Actions, Shapes, Places, Toys, Emotions.

## Safety Design
- No open child chatbot.
- AI functions are teacher-controlled and local simulated logic.
- Admin AI Safety Review queue.
- Parent-safe reporting.
- No ads and no external links required.
